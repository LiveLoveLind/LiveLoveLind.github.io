Wedding Rehearsals - $50

At LiveLoveLind, we've separated out our pricing for rehearsals for the convenience of our couples. We recognize not every wedding requires or desires one! So we leave it up to you. However, I always recommend having a rehearsal. It's a great way to ensure your bridal party knows where to stand, how to face the camera, and various other tips that ensure a smooth processional. It also gives us a chance to change up any last minute details so that the day is just as you want it!

This package includes:

  • Talk through: I spend the first part introducing myself to the party and talking them through your ceremony. I will not read the script word for word, we'll save that for the day! But I highlight cues they need to know, and the general elements that will take place.

  • Walk through: Next I guide the party through all the motions at 1/2 speed. I promise the first walk through will look awkward! But, this will get out all the confusion.

  • Final Walk through: Now that everyone knows where they need to be, we repeat the processional from the beginning and answer any final questions.

  • This should take between 30- 45 minutes depending on the number of party members and complexity of the ceremony.
