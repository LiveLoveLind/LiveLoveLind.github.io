Why choose us for your wedding?

01

The Wedding Workshop

Planning a ceremony can be daunting! Not surprisingly, most couples aren't sure what all they should or could include. In my 1 hour Wedding Workshop I walk my couples through all the standard elements of a ceremony and we capture all of the important details: cast of characters, logisitcs, and any must-have or must-avoid details. Don't have all the answers on day 1? Don't worry! The Workshop kicks off the creative process, but we can make changes and updates right up to the big day.

02

Your Wedding, Your Script

You can have peace of mind knowing what will be said, word-for-word, on the big day. 24/7 access to your wedding ceremony script via Google docs allows us to collaberate throughout the process and gives you total control over the final draft.

Want to keep your vows a surprise? No problem! I love a good surprise and am happy to create separate documents for the vows and work with you both privately.
